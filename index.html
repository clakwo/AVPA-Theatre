The Best Academy at BCA filled with the best people you'll meet in you're life! We are theatre and theatre is life. Therefore, we are Life. According to the Transitive Property of Addition ;)

This is an awesome place where you can find people with the same interests as you. You learn how to improve in your acting, your singing, and your dancing. You also get to learn a lot about how theater started and once in a while you will have a class where all you do is wear wigs and yell in Japanese.

One thing that is important and awesome about the theatre academy at BCA is that you will get extremely close to the students in your theatre class. It is a relatively small class and you will all learn to come together and love each other through your 4 years together!

Theatre kids have "theatre families" that have been here since the beginning of AVPA/T classes. You're parent will be someone from the grade one above you, your grandparent will be 2 years above you, and great grandparent will be a senior in your time. Families can become extremely close and the love will radiate from your families. Families are awesome for support, advice, and just some extra love.
